Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about CareerLift AI.

What is an ATS and why does it matter? ▼
An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by 99% of Fortune 500 companies to automatically scan, filter, and rank CVs before a human ever sees them. If your CV isn't formatted correctly or lacks the right keywords, it gets rejected automatically — even if you're perfectly qualified for the role.
How does the ATS CV Rewriter work? ▼
You upload your existing CV and enter the target job title, country, and optionally the job description. Our AI analyzes your CV against ATS requirements for your specific market, rewrites it with the right keywords and formatting, and gives you a before/after ATS score so you can see the improvement.
Is my data private and secure? ▼
Yes. Your CV data is used solely to generate your optimized output and is never shared with third parties, sold, or stored long-term. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR and international data privacy standards.
Which countries and job markets does CareerLift AI support? ▼
CareerLift AI supports 100+ countries including the USA, UK, Canada, Germany, Australia, UAE, Netherlands, Singapore, India, and many more. The AI adapts its rewriting to match local CV conventions, ATS systems, and recruiter expectations specific to each market.
Can I use CareerLift AI for free? ▼
Yes! You get 3 free AI analyses when you create an account — no credit card required. This includes CV rewrites, LinkedIn optimization, and cover letter generation. The ATS Score Checker is always free with no credit limit.
What file formats can I upload? ▼
You can upload PDF, DOCX, DOC, or TXT files (up to 5 MB). You can also paste your CV text directly into the text area. Our AI extracts the text automatically from uploaded files.
How is the LinkedIn Optimizer different from editing my profile manually? ▼
The LinkedIn Optimizer uses AI trained on recruiter behavior and LinkedIn's search algorithm to craft headlines and summaries with the exact keywords recruiters search for. It gives you 3 headline options and a fully rewritten About section optimized for your target role and country.
What happens after my free credits run out? ▼
You can upgrade to one of our paid plans — Basic ($19/mo), Pro ($49/mo), or a one-time credit pack. Paid plans include unlimited or expanded analyses, priority processing, and access to all premium templates. The ATS Score Checker remains free forever on all plans.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

CareerLift AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your name and email address when you register
  • CV / resume content: text you paste or upload for analysis (processed in memory; not stored permanently)
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, browser type, IP address (anonymised)
  • Payment data: handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or store card numbers

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our AI-powered CV analysis services
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To send transactional emails (OTP codes, receipts) — never marketing without consent
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Retention

CV text submitted for analysis is processed in real time and not stored beyond your session. Account metadata is retained while your account is active. Request deletion at privacy@careerliftai.com.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database (EU region)
  • Anthropic / Claude API — AI analysis engine
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Netlify — website hosting

5. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for authentication. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7. Contact

Email privacy@careerliftai.com or use our contact form.
