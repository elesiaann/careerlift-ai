Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

What is an ATS and why does my CV score matter?+
An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by 99% of large employers to automatically filter CVs before a human ever reads them. It scores your CV based on keyword matching, formatting, and completeness against the job description. A low ATS score means your CV is likely rejected automatically — even if you're perfectly qualified. CareerLift AI optimizes your CV to pass these filters and reach the recruiter's desk.
How does the AI CV Rewriter work?+
You upload your existing CV (PDF or paste text) and enter the job title and target country. Our AI analyzes your content, identifies missing keywords, restructures your experience for ATS readability, and rewrites your bullet points to be results-focused and market-specific. The entire process takes under 60 seconds and produces a fully rewritten CV ready to download.
Which countries and job markets are supported?+
CareerLift AI is optimized for the USA, UK, Germany, Canada, Australia, UAE, the Netherlands, and many more. Each market has different hiring norms — for example, German CVs include a photo and date of birth, while UK/US CVs do not. Our AI automatically adapts your CV to match local expectations and recruiter preferences for the market you select.
What does the LinkedIn Optimizer do differently?+
The LinkedIn Optimizer focuses on discoverability and recruiter appeal — two things a standard CV rewrite doesn't cover. It rewrites your headline with high-search keywords, crafts a compelling About summary in first-person voice, and restructures your experience entries to match LinkedIn's algorithm. The result is a profile that ranks higher in recruiter searches and makes a stronger first impression.
Can I generate multiple cover letters for different jobs?+
Yes. Each cover letter is generated fresh based on the specific job description you provide. You can paste any job posting and the AI will tailor the letter to that exact role — matching tone, keywords, and company context. Pro users can generate unlimited cover letters, while Free users receive 3 credits to get started.
What's included in the free plan?+
The free plan includes 3 AI analysis credits that can be used across any tool — CV rewrite, ATS score check, cover letter, or LinkedIn optimization. You also get access to all CV templates at no cost. Credits never expire, so you can use them whenever you're ready. Upgrade to a paid plan for unlimited access and priority processing.
Is my CV data kept private and secure?+
Yes. Your CV and personal data are never sold or shared with third parties. Analysis is processed in real-time and your data is not used to train AI models. All data is stored with industry-standard encryption. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings.
How quickly can I expect to see results?+
Most users report receiving interview invitations within 1–3 weeks of using an optimized CV. Results depend on the job market and role you're applying to, but on average our users see a +44 point ATS score increase and an 89% interview callback rate. Combining the CV rewriter, ATS checker, LinkedIn optimizer, and a tailored cover letter gives you the best possible chance of getting noticed quickly.
